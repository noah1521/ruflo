## Playbook #4 — ETF Exclusion Rule (OTM Call Scanner)

**Documented**: 2026-06-09 after full 8-ticker scan

### Pattern
ETFs (BOTZ, SOXL, SPCX) consistently fail the Z&N OTM call criteria by structure:
- Criterion 2 (Earnings Beat) = always N/A — no earnings
- Criterion 3 (Activist/M&A) = always N/A — ETFs are not acquisition targets
- Criterion 4 (Options Liquidity) = frequently fails — thinly traded or wide spreads (especially SPCX)
- Leveraged ETFs (SOXL) carry additional structural put-hedging demand that biases P/C ratio toward FAIL

### Rule
**DO NOT run ETFs through the Z&N OTM call criteria scanner.** They cannot satisfy criteria 2 and 3 by definition. Thinly traded ETFs (SPCX, BOTZ) also frequently fail criterion 4 on liquidity. ETFs belong to Playbook #2 (Equity Accumulation) only.

### Exceptions
- If a sector ETF (BOTZ, SOXL) is near a confirmed technical breakout AND options liquidity is verified live, a directional call spread may be considered — but this is NOT the Playbook #1 OTM call setup.

- They dominate criteria 1 (consistently near ATH), 2 (overwhelming analyst upgrades), and 5 (bullish sentiment)
